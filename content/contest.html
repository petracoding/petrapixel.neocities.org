Petra's 2nd Indie Web Contest!

My last indie web contest was a celebration for me reaching 300 followers on Neocities. As I now have more than 1300 1800 followers I think it's time for another one!

I see so many beautiful, interesting, unique, and helpful websites on the indie web, and the point of this contest to celebrate them!

Vote now!
You can use demonicpedigree's spreadsheet to help you if you want.
Voting will be open until at least December 12th.
Go tell your friends + followers to vote!!!

Rules


Voting Rules:
  • Only fill out the voting form once! (If you forgot whether you have already voted you can ask me.)
  • While voting, think about which category you're currently voting for!
  • You don't have to have participated to vote.
  • Do not vote for yourself.
  • Don't award "friendship points" or give someone fewer points because you disagree with their views.

Participation Rules:
Show/Hide
  • Your website should be coded and created by you.
    • No social media profiles, carrds, websites from drag-and-drop generators, etc.
    • No AI-generated code or main page content. (The odd AI-generated image is ok, especially if criticized)
    • No out-of-the-box layouts/templates are allowed (including mine). Layouts based on template generators or similar tools are allowed as long as you've added your own touch to them by changing the appearance enough. This means they shouldn't look like the default output of the layout generator you used. Basically, if I can tell which generator you used, it's probably not allowed.
  • Your website should not be empty / completely unfinished. This means, for example, you should have at least 2 subpages with content. This is not a hard rule, so use your own discretion please. If you're unsure, just participate, I will disqualify you if I think your website's not finished enough.
    • Please remove any links that don't work yet (e.g. those that lead to empty "work in progress" pages) from your navigation for the duration of the contest. This is to make voting easier for everyone.
  • Your website can be hosted anywhere; it does not have to be hosted on Neocities.
  • If you have a concrete follower count of your website (e.g. on Neocities or Nekoweb) please only participate if you have fewer than 300 followers at the time of participating. (It's fine if you go over the limit while the contest is ongoing.) Similarly, if you don't have a concrete follower count but know that you have a very popular site, please do not participate, as the point of this contest is to give "unknown gems" a chance to shine!
  • No hate content or discrimination of any kind. If I see such content on your site you will be disqualified.
  • No corporate websites, CVs, etc. This contest is for indie websites.
  • NSFW/18+ and triggering content is allowed, but please check the appropriate warning checkbox in the participation form.
  • Thanks for reading the rules! The password for the participation form is cat.
  • Please also participate in the voting later! The contest won't work if there are not enough voters!

If you're not sure about any of the rules just shoot me a message or comment below.

Evaluation

Each voter will give websites 1-5 points (5 being the best). At the end of the voting stage I will calculate the average points each website received. (I use average and not total so that it does not matter how many voters each website has received, as not every voter votes for every website. Website links in the voting form are in a randomized order to keep things fair.)

Each category will have 3 winners.

Prizes

There are no physical prizes, but your website will be featured on this page in perpetuity once the winners are decided, and you can feel really proud of yourself! Every winner will get a little winner badge for their website (in the form of a 88x31 button and a larger image). As a bonus, every winner can request any kind of tutorial/resource from me (though I obviously can't promise that I will be able to do it because I don't know everything, but I will try).

If you're interested in offering a prize - no matter how small or silly - please contact me through the comment section below or my guestbook, we'll make it happen!
