Petra's 2nd Indie Web Contest!

My last indie web contest was a celebration for me reaching 300 followers on Neocities. As I now have more than 1300 followers I think it's time for another one!

I see so many beautiful, interesting, unique, and helpful websites on the indie web, and the point of this contest to celebrate them!

Participation form opens soon!

Rules


Participation Rules:
  • Your website should be coded by you.
    • No social media profiles, carrds, etc.
    • No AI-generated code or main page content. (The odd AI-generated image is ok.)
    • No out-of-the-box layouts/templates are allowed (including mine). However, layouts based on template generators or similar tools are allowed as long as you've added your own touch to them by changing the appearance enough. This means they shouldn't look like the default output of the layout generator you used. Basically, if I can tell which generator you used, it's probably not allowed.
  • Your website should not be empty or under heavy construction. This means you should have at least 2 subpages with content. Homepages with no working links in the navigation do not count as finished. Please remove any links that don't work yet from your navigation.
  • Your website can be hosted anywhere; it does not have to be hosted on Neocities.
  • If you have a concrete follower count of your website (e.g. on Neocities or Nekoweb) please only participate if you have fewer than 300 followers at the time of participating. (It's fine if you go over the limit while the contest is ongoing.) Similarly, if you don't have a concrete follower count but know that you have a very popular site, please do not participate, as the point of this contest is to give "unknown gems" a chance to shine!
  • No hate content or discrimination of any kind. If I see such content on your site you will be disqualified.
  • No corporate websites, CVs, etc. This contest is for indie websites.
  • NSFW/18+ is okay, but please check the NSFW/18+ checkbox in the participation form.
  • Please also participate in the voting later! The contest won't work if there are not enough voters!

If you're not sure about any of the rules just shoot me a message or comment below.

Evaluation

Before voting opens I will explain here how evaluation will work. It will probably be based on average points. (I can't do it with total points because not every voter rates every website.)

Prizes

There are no physical prizes, but your website will be featured on this page in perpetuity once the winners are decided, and you can feel really proud of yourself! Every winner will get a little winner badge for their website (in the form of a 88x31 button and a larger image). As a bonus, every winner can request any kind of tutorial/resource from me (though I obviously can't promise that I will be able to do it, but I will try).
