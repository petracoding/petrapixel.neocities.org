Advent Calendar of Digital Challenges!

I thought it would be fun to create a little advent calendar for all you folks! Every door will have a digital challenge that has something to do with your phone, pc, the internet, software, etc. Most challenges are about improving your digital experience/home. Some can be done in 5 minutes, some might take an hour or longer. There's no need to do every single one - though I recommend it! No challenges will be specific to having your own website, so you can participate even if you don't have a website!

Real life is keeping me REALLY busy so the calendar might not be pretty, but it will be there!

(Doors can only be opened if it's actually that day or later (in your timezone) - no cheating!)

Please come back in December!
I'm still working on the calendar...
