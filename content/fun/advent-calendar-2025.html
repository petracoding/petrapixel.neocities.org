Advent Calendar of Digital Challenges!

I thought it would be fun to create a little advent calendar for all you folks! Every door will have a digital challenge that has something to do with your phone, pc, the internet, software, etc. Most challenges are about improving your digital experience/home. Some can be done in 5 minutes, some might take an hour or longer. There's no need to do every single one - though I recommend it! No challenges will be specific to having your own website, so you can participate even if you don't have a website! If you enjoy this calendar please share it with others :3 Btw, the doors can only be opened if it's actually that day (in your timezone) or later. Happy Holidays! (Background illustration by samantha grace.)

Please note there are only 24 doors because that's how it is in my country, we celebrate on the 24th :)

Please come back in December!
