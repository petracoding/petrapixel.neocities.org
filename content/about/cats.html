Last autumn I took in a stray cat that gave birth to 5 kittens in my apartment. As an unemployed uni student, the expenses were rough for me. (200€ for raising the kittens, over 1600€ for castration due to complications. ) I appreciate any help on my ko-fi, no matter how small!

If you have questions about this page feel free to contact me via my guestbook or my neocities profile. I promise I don't bite!

Holly

Name: Holly

Gender: female

Age: unknown, but ~1 and a half years

Colour: tabby with white spots

Likes: cuddles. a lot. <3

Dislikes: Pushi (the neighbor's cat)

i met her on: july 31st 2025

Holly is a stray that loves to visit me my cat! She was very shy at the start, but once I managed to lure her in with snacks and pet her she realised that she actually loves cuddles! She then started visiting my apartment every day and it soon felt like she was actually 'my' cat. I love her so much, and now she's officially mine!!! I still can't believe I have a cat, and such a lovely one!

On October 10th she gave birth to 5 kittens in my apartment. So now I'm a grandma. Thanks, Holly. I was not ready for this responsibility. One of the kittens (Dobby) unfortunately didn't make it through the first night due to health issues. The four others (Itsy, Bitsy, Teenie and Weenie) are healthy and almost all grown up! They now have new, loving homes, and I get lots of picture updates, which I love!

After all the kittens were rehomed it was time to castrate Holly. Unfortunately, due to some complications, she almost died, and had to stay at the vet for several days. The whole thing cost me over 1600 bucks but every cent was worth it because now I have her back, happy & healthy. Since the operation she has become even cuddlier. She goes to sleep on top of me whenever I lay down in bed, and at night she goes to sleep with me, often still sleeping on me when I wake up in the morning. It's so adorable and I'm so lucky!

Because she was a stray and is used to being outside she's still an outdoor cat now. This suits me well because I have a tiny apartment she would otherwise be bored in. However, I tried to make it interesting to her by placing cat shelves on my wall that she can climb. She prefers to nap on the bed though.

I also like to go on walks with Holly with her on a leash. Currently just through the garden, but I hope we can go on longer walks soon. (She's still skittish around even the smaller roads next to the garden.)

Holly is just a really special cat, and I'm so happy she chose me to be her owner. She's my baby.
